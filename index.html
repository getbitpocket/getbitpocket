Enabling Bitcoin Payments at the Point of Sale.
What is Bitcoin?

Bitcoin is a global digital currency. Bitcoin is based on peer-to-peer internet technology and thus, not controlled by any central authority. Transaction processing and currency issuance is organized completely dezentralized.

What is BitPocket?

BitPocket aims to provide a mobile app for merchants, in order to accept Bitcoin as a means of Payment at their Point of Sale. Accepting electronic payments was never easier.
